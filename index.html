Who I Am

Hello! My name is Jimmy and I am a software engineer (L60) at Microsoft, Inc. Previously, I worked as a software development engineer at Amazon on their Advertising Privacy team and in May 2020, I completed my undergrad at the University of Rochester with bachelor degrees in Computer Science (B.S.) and Business (B.A.) and a minor in Chinese. At school, you could've found me bunkered in the library working with others on class projects or in the front of the classroom leading workshops and grading exams for the "Data Structures & Algorithms" course on campus. Last summer, I moved to Seattle, WA to start my new role at Microsoft.

Outside of work and school, I like to build up new programming skills, tinker with my own side projects, play sports like badminton, tennis, and volleyball and spend time with family and friends.
